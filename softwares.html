Since the principles of the Folding Studio are the freedom to create, study, and share accurate representations of molecules, all graphics are derived from open-source software. Here are a few examples:

UCSF Chimera
Chimera is a program for the interactive visualization and analysis of molecular structures and related data.
Gromacs
Gromacs is a versatile package to perform molecular dynamics, i.e. simulate the Newtonian equations of motion for systems with hundreds to millions of particles.
Blender

GIMP
GIMP is an acronym for GNU Image Manipulation Program. It is a freely distributed program for such tasks as photo retouching, image composition, and image authoring.
